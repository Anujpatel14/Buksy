# Buksy Overview

Buksy is a local personal operating system designed to manage your everyday work and life. It integrates a variety of productivity, planning, and analytical tools into a single, cohesive application that runs locally on your machine, prioritizing privacy and local-first data processing.

## Core Idea
The core idea behind Buksy is to go beyond simple task management by incorporating behavior-aware recommendations, predictive scheduling, and deep integrations. It acts as a "digital twin," learning from your habits, energy levels, and focus patterns to intelligently suggest what to do next. By simulating future outcomes and analyzing past behaviors, Buksy helps optimize your daily schedule, manage multiple projects efficiently, and prevent burnout. 

## Key Features

- **Next-Step Suggestions:** Analyzes urgency, energy levels, duration, and goal/project pressure to recommend the best next task.
- **Advanced Scheduling:** Features a daily plan and multi-project schedule with due dates, deadlines, carry-forward logic, and capacity-aware day buckets.
- **Behavioral Insights:** Includes check-ins (energy, focus, mood) that automatically adjust your workload, along with habit DNA and emotional intelligence tracking to detect burnout cycles and hidden triggers.
- **Digital Twin & Predictive Day:** Simulates your day to provide cognitive load and focus guard signals, estimating follow-through and "what will I likely do?" outcomes based on your decision style.
- **Future Timeline Engine:** Projects future paths (aggressive, balanced, lazy) based on your current workload and completion velocity.
- **Autonomous Action Center:** Plans actions like follow-up emails and scheduling, offering a "Life Autopilot" that previews day rebalancing and trims overload with an approval queue.
- **Life Analytics Dashboard:** Displays performance windows, avoidance patterns, goal success probabilities, and delay diagnoses.
- **Chat & Voice Integration:** Offers an optional Ollama-powered chat for natural language routing and a voice command center for hands-free planning and execution.
- **Thinking Studio & Workbench:** Provides a space for multi-agent analysis, role switching, research briefs, file analysis, and knowledge queries.
- **Deep Integrations:** Connects seamlessly with Jira, GitHub, Notion, and Google Calendar, alongside a plugin hub for extended local functionality.
